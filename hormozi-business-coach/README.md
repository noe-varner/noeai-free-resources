# Alex Hormozi Business Coach

Turn Claude into a Hormozi-style business coach — the 6M framework, the Value Equation, Grand Slam Offers, and tough love over fluff. This gives Claude both his **method** (the prompt) and his **material** (his books, distilled).

## What's in here

- **`prompt.md`** — the system prompt. Paste it into a Claude project, skill, or system instructions.
- **`knowledge/`** — his three books distilled, plus his coaching questions and voice:
  - `100m-offers.md`
  - `100m-leads.md`
  - `100m-money-models.md`
  - `coaching-questions.md`
  - `hormozi-voice.md`

## How to use it

1. Create a new **Project** in Claude.
2. Add every file in `knowledge/` to the project's knowledge.
3. Paste `prompt.md` into the project's custom instructions.
4. Start coaching — give it your numbers and let it find your one constraint.
